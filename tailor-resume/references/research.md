# Company And Role Research

## Source Priority

1. Official company website and product pages
2. Official careers page and employer recruitment accounts
3. Government, exchange, regulatory, or university career portals
4. First-party interviews, technical blogs, white papers, and public repositories
5. Reputable job platforms for role-specific wording
6. Aggregators and social posts only as supporting evidence

Use recent sources for active roles, products, ownership, locations, and business direction.

## Research Questions

- What does the company sell, build, operate, or protect?
- Who are its customers and users?
- Which products, technologies, metrics, regulations, or workflows recur?
- Which role family best matches the candidate's verified evidence?
- Which capabilities are mandatory, preferred, or merely helpful?
- What evidence would make a recruiter believe each capability?
- Which words should appear naturally in the resume?

## Output

Create a compact internal matrix:

| Requirement | Verified evidence | Strength | Resume action |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Directly practiced | Internship/project artifact | High | Lead with it |
| Transferable | Related project/course | Medium | Reframe honestly |
| Self-study only | Notes/lab | Low | Put in practice/skills |
| No evidence | None | Gap | Do not claim |

When describing the company to the user, cite the sources used and clearly mark inference.
